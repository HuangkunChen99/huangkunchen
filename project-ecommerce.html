Project Overview

This project analyzes cross-border e-commerce trends using large-scale product data from Tmall Global. Through data visualization, correlation analysis, and regression modeling, we gained key insights into sales behavior, consumer sentiment, and market strategy across major product categories such as beauty, health, and digital goods.

Key Highlights

  • Processed and cleaned large datasets (200K+ records) with pricing, reviews, and emotional scores
  • Analyzed product pricing trends, sentiment scores, and category-level behavior
  • Built regression and PCA models to quantify key drivers of sales and sentiment
  • Identified high-performing suppliers and regional market differences based on shipping data

Results

The analysis provided actionable recommendations for product pricing strategy and marketing focus. It highlighted strong correlations between sentiment scores and product sales, especially in high-demand categories. PCA regression helped simplify variable selection and improved model performance.
