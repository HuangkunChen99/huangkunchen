Project Overview

This project simulates the Microsoft Viva Engage environment to measure digital community health in enterprise collaboration platforms. Our team built AI models using Reddit data and sentiment analysis to construct a Civility Index and Community Health Index (CHI)â€”measuring quality and vitality of internal social platforms.

  • Applied insights to simulate internal enterprise social behavior and flag early warning signals

Technologies Used

  • Power BI
  • Prompt engineering and sentiment analytics
